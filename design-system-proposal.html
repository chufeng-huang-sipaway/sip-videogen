Usage Rules

  • Primary actions: Brand 500 background, white text
  • Hover states: Brand 600 or Brand 20% overlay
  • Selected/Active: Brand 10% background, Brand 600 text
  • Destructive actions: Brand 500 (red works for delete/danger)
  • Success states: Success green only
  • Warnings: Brand 10% background (amber replaced)
  • Info/neutral: Neutral 100 background
  • Errors: Brand 500 text (red = error is intuitive)
